FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are generic medicines safe? ▾

Yes, absolutely. Generic medicines contain the same active pharmaceutical ingredients (API) as branded drugs and are manufactured under WHO-GMP certified conditions. They are tested in NABL-accredited labs and approved by the Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation (CDSCO).

Why are generic medicines so much cheaper? ▾

Branded medicines include the cost of R&D, marketing, and brand premium. Generic medicines skip these costs since the patent has expired and they don't need heavy advertising — the savings are passed directly to you.

Do I need a prescription? ▾

For prescription medicines (Schedule H and Schedule H1), a valid doctor's prescription is required. Over-the-counter medicines can be purchased without a prescription. Our pharmacist will guide you.

Can I bring my branded medicine prescription and get a generic? ▾

Yes! Our pharmacist can help identify the generic equivalent of any branded medicine. The active ingredient, strength, and dosage will be the same — just the price will be different (much lower!).

What are the store timings? ▾

We are open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We are closed on national holidays. Please call us for Sunday availability.
